Fluffy Pancakes

+

Perfect for weekend brunch. Light, fluffy, and simple to make.

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps: Mix dry ingredients, add wet, whisk, and cook on a skillet until golden.

Fresh Guacamole

+

Creamy avocado dip perfect with chips or as a topping.

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup onion
  • Lime juice
  • Salt & pepper

Steps: Mash avocados, mix all ingredients, serve fresh and cold.
